<s id="the-gold-Apple" title="Who will get the gold apple?">

[

Paris of Troy ( @wilw ) is shepherding a flock of muppet sheep.

Aphrodite ( <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jennifer_Saunders">Jennifer Saunders</a> - like in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Absolutely_Fabulous">Absolutely Fabulous</a> ), and
Athena ( <a href="https://twitter.com/TiffanyAlvord">Tiffany Alvord</a> ),
and Hera approach.

]

Arielle's Voice: so, the three goddesses approached Paris to decide who'll get
the gold apple.

Hera: what?! Why do I need one? I've got too much stuff around the house as it
is.

Aphrodite and Athena: yeah!

Arielle's Voice: ok, then let's say it's a delicious chocolate apple with
a golden wrapping.

[

The goddesses think for a moment, and then start making offers.

Cut.

]

Paris: OK, so Hera's offering me political power. I don't want that,
because I've got enough problems managing the sheep here.

A Muppet sheep: damn right he does! He's so bad that we've been talking about
forming a union!

Paris: yes, and I'd rather not take Athena's offer of knowledge, because
one needs to invest effort in acquiring it.

+++: But getting laid sounds good, so I'll go with Aphrodite.

Aphrodite: good call, Paris baby! So you want the fairest damsel in the
land?

Paris: nah… <a href="https://www.shlomifish.org/humour/image-macros/indiv-nodes/it_is_too_easy.xhtml">too easy</a>! I want a woman who is the 2nd-fairest in
the same room as Kermit the Frog.

Aphrodite: fair enough… [she takes the apple and unwraps it.]

+++: hmmm… it's pretty large. Let's split it into 3 parts!

[ Hera and Athena sigh and shake their heads. ]

</s>

<s id="the-second-fairest" title="Matching Paris and Helen">

[ Based on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=x_hoV9pCoXA">this Sesame Street sketch</a> ]

Kermit: and here is Helen the evil witch's magic mirror. Mr. mirror,
what do you expect her to ask you this time?

Magic Mirror: same question as always: "Who is the fairest of them all?"

[ Helen enters the frame. ]

Helen: hi, Kermit.

+++: mirror, mirror, on the wall: who's the fairest in this room?

Magic Mirror: Kermit the Frog.

Helen: figures.

+++: mirror, mirror, on the wall: who's the second fairest in this room?

Magic Mirror: you.

[ Paris enters the frame. ]

Paris: wow, she is kinda cute: the googly eyes… the long nose… the retro
hat… the green muppety skin.

Helen: thanks, that's the nicest thing anyone ever said to me.

[ Helen uses her smartphone to start a video chat with
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Menelaus">Menelaus</a> of Sparta,
her husband. ]

Helen: hey, Menelaus, baby! Sorry, but it's over - I'm with Paris now.

Menelaus: with… a girl?

Helen: no, silly, prince Paris of Troy. Say “hi”, Paris.

Paris: hi! [he waves.]

Menelaus: oh, I see. Very well. But I hope you're going to help us break
the record for the number of ships that were launched by a single face.

Paris: you mean the <a href="https://livexp.com/blog/what-is-the-face-that-launched-a-thousand-ships/">face that launched a thousand ships</a>?

Menelaus: actually, we're up to 5,372 ships.

Helen: count me in! Kermit, would you like to tag along and document it?

Kermit: sure - I'm game.

</s>

<s id="achilles-heel" title="Shooting Achilles">

Arielle: Where’s Achilles?

[

<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kevin_Sorbo">Kevin Sorbo</a>,
who played Hercules in
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hercules:_The_Legendary_Journeys">Hercules: The Legendary Journeys</a>,
enters the frame, while wearing a similar skimpy outfit.

Applause.

]

Arielle: hey, didn't you play Hercules?

Achilles: yeah, but I'm getting paid enough to let it slide.

Arielle: OK, Paris, shoot Achilles' heel.

Athena: hey, can I do it instead this time?

Arielle: but aren't you on Achilles' side?

Athena: yeah, but we're getting paid enough to let it slide.

Arielle: fair enough.

[

The pumpkin muppets from <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Qj8PhxSnhg">the "Cårven Der Pümpkîn | Recipes with The Swedish Chef | The Muppets - YouTube" sketch</a>
only wearing <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Che_Guevara">Che Guevara</a>-like
berets enter.

]

Pumpkin muppet: hey Athena babe, you don't wanna use a bow-and-arrow. They
are kinda old-fashioned.

+++: you wanna be a modern fighter… do modern warfare.

+++: you wanna use… a bazooka!

Athena: a bazooka! Sounds good.

Pumpkin muppet: excellent. Let's summon our bazooka instructor.

+++: hey, Chef, come here.

[

The Swedish Chef enters carrying a bazooka.

]

Chef: hindeh-hoven de-bazooka.

Achilles: OK, ok, I'm not getting paid enough to be blown-to-bits by
a deranged chick using a bazooka with a mock-Swedish-speaking instructor.
[ <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1f3b_hLVYGA">Reference: "mock-Swedish"</a>. ]

Pumpkin muppet: hey pal, for your information, mock-Swedish is one of the most
beautiful mock-languages of mock-Scandinavia.

Achilles: whatever! Please bring my cardboard double. [he walks over to
Athena's other side.]

Chef: to usen de-bazookan, aimen de-bazooka und shooten de-bazooka.

[ Athena shoots, and blows up Achilles' cardboard double. ]

Achilles: wow! That was a great shot!

[

He grabs Athena and kisses her on the lips prolongedly.

]

Achilles: [to @wilw/Paris] hey, sorry, man! Couldn't resist.

@wilw: no worries.
