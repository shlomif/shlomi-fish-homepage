9.2 toc

  • toc automatically creates a table of contents from the <h1>, <h2>, etc. headers found in the HTML. 

#use wml::std::toc
#use wml::std::page

<page />
<h1>My Page</h1>

<p>
This is my page
</p>

<h2>Section 1</h2>

<p>
This is section 1
</p>

<h2>Section 2</h2>

<p>
This is section 2
</p>

<h3>Sub-section 2.1</h3>

<p>
This is a subsection
</p>

<toc />
