[

Athena ( <a href="https://twitter.com/TiffanyAlvord">Tiffany Alvord</a> ),
Dionysus ( <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jack_Black">Jack Black</a> ),
Artemis ( <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a> ),
and Arielle (= the tenth muse, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regina_King">Regina King</a>), enter a large hall of a pub / restaurant hosting a board games' event.

Athena is wearing a T-shirt with the caption “My father accumulated too much wisdom, so he begot me. @Athena”

]

Athena: so that's it…

[

<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wil_Wheaton">Wil Wheaton</a>, wearing
a T-shirt captioned “Try to be less of a dick than me. #dontbeadick @wilw”,
approaches.

]

wilw: Hey, Athena! I'm sorry about the other day… you and your friends look
great today… I'll be over there if you need anything.

[

He walks to a nearby table.

]

Artemis: oh my god! Wil Wheaton??! Women would kill to kiss him.

Arielle: Athena, that is one helluva way <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/howyoudoin/comments/m0bv5i/ross_in_s04e07_yeah_like_i_could_lose_it/">to lose it</a>!

+++: Now, go talk to him.

[ Athena approaches wilw. ]

Athena: So Mr. Wheaton… out of pure curiosity… are you single?

wilw: Well, in real life, I'm happily married. But, in this fic, I'm single.

Athena: <a href="http://shlomifishswiki.branchable.com/99_Problems/">You've got 99 problems</a> …

wilw: … and a bitchin' , sexy, significant other like you ain't one! Yes!!
