The Project's Purpose

The purpose of the project is to program a noise simulator for TCP/IP noise, which will deliberatly drop and hold IP packets that pass through the computer. The simulator will support controlled noise, such as a host-based noise, or one with a time-varying behaviour.

The incensitive for this is to allow researchers and developers to test whether their protocols are immune to various types of network noise.
